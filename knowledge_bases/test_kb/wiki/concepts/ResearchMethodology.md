# Research Methodology

## Overview
Research methodology refers to the systematic process or framework used to conduct research, collect data, and analyze findings. It encompasses the overall approach and design of a study.

## Key Principles
- Systematic data collection and analysis
- Clear documentation of processes
- Reproducibility of results
